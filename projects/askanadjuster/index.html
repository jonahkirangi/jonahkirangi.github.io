Ask An Adjuster

Project Description

Auto insurance claims are confusing. Ask An Adjuster is a message-based service that allows people to send their personal claims questions directly to a team of independent auto adjusters and appraisers. The team then reviews and replies directly to each message with unbiased and fact-based information. Ask An Adjuster's purpose is to make customers knowledgeable about their auto or personal injury claim.
