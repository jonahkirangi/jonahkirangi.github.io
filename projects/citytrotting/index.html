City Trotting

Project Description

Don't be a tourist. Travel like a local. City Trotting allows travelers to easily find a local guide in any city in the world. Select a guide based upon language compatibility, unique expertise, or member ranking. Travelers can review and rate their City Trotting guides on a 5 star scale. Guides can also rate travelers.
